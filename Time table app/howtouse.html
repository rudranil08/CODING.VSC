This is how to use the app


First enter all your Tasks by typing them in ADD TASK


Then click the calander and then enter the date you want to complete the work


Click the time button and then select the time or deadline that the work has to be done by


If no time is required just put a random time


Then add the task


After your done with the task there will be a small checkbox in the left hand side of the task click that and then click on clear completed.
